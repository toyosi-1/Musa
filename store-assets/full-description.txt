Musa – Modern Estate Access Control

Say goodbye to manual gatehouse logs, lost visitor slips, and long queues at the gate. Musa is a smart, fast, and secure access control system built for residential estates. Whether you're a resident expecting a guest or a guard verifying entry, Musa makes the entire process seamless.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FOR RESIDENTS

• Generate Access Codes – Create unique text-based or QR access codes for your guests in seconds. Each code is tied to your household and can be set to expire automatically.

• Share Instantly – Send codes directly to your visitors via any messaging app. They simply present the code at the gate.

• Household Management – Add family members and housemates to your household. Everyone can generate and manage access codes under one roof.

• Real-Time Notifications – Get instant alerts when your access code is scanned at the gate, so you always know when your guest arrives.

• Activity History – View a complete log of all access events linked to your household — who came, when, and which gate.

• Community Feed – Stay connected with your neighbours. Post updates, share announcements, and discuss estate matters in a built-in community feed.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FOR SECURITY GUARDS

• Verify Codes Instantly – Enter a text code or scan a QR code to verify visitor access in seconds. No paperwork, no delays.

• QR Scanner – Use the built-in camera scanner to read QR codes directly — fast, accurate, and hands-free.

• Verification Dashboard – See your shift stats at a glance: approved entries, denied attempts, and total verifications for the day.

• Activity Log – Keep a detailed record of every verification you perform for accountability and audit purposes.

• Privacy First – Guards verify access without seeing the resident's personal details. The system confirms validity without exposing who generated the code.
