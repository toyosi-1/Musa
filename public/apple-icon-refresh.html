Musa Icon Update

To update the Musa app icon on your home screen, please follow these steps:

  1. Remove the current Musa app from your home screen
  2. Clear Safari cache by going to Settings > Safari > Clear History and Website Data
  3. Visit the Musa app again in Safari
  4. Add to Home Screen using the share button

You should now see the updated Musa icon on your home screen!
